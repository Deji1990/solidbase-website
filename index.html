Risk 01

Demolition without warning.

Structures on drainage alignments, road setbacks, or protected corridors are routinely cleared. No compensation. No appeal window.

Risk 02

Government acquisition.

Vast tracts across Lagos are committed to state or federal use — often decades before signage appears. The land is no longer the seller's to sell.

Risk 03

Dispute & double-sale.

A single plot can be "sold" to three buyers by unverified omoniles. By the time court decides, years and legal fees have evaporated.
